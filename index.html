How It Works

Three steps to your story

No account needed. No data sent to third parties. Just your history, visualised.

01
Export from Netflix

Go to your Netflix account → Viewing Activity → Download All. You'll get a CSV file with your full watching history.

02
Upload & Unwrap

Drop your CSV into the My Dashboard tab. Instantly see your top shows, genre breakdown, binge days, and monthly patterns all in one dashboard.

03
Compare & Connect

Compare your taste with friends, explore community stats, and join discussions with other Netflix watchers who share your obsessions.
